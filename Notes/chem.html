Periodicity

Increasing atomic number results in an increasing nuclear charge
Increasing number of occupied electron shells results in an increasing screening effect

  • Covalent radius: Decreases across a period (nuclear charge), Increases down a group (Occupied electron shells)
  • First Ionisation E: Increases across a period (nuclear charge), Decreases down a group (Screening effect)
  • Electronegativity: Increases across a period, Decreases down a group (Screening effect)
